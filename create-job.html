How Your Job Posting Works

The information you provide will help match qualified candidates to your position. Our system analyzes applicant resumes against your job requirements to identify the best matches and streamline your hiring process.

Basic Information

Choose a clear, descriptive title that candidates will search for

Specify the work location or indicate if the role is remote

Job Description

Provide a clear overview of what the role involves and day-to-day responsibilities

List specific tasks and duties. Use bullet points for clarity

Requirements

Separate skills with commas. These will be used to match candidates

Nice-to-have skills that would be beneficial but aren't required

List any additional requirements or qualifications

Compensation & Benefits

Providing salary range increases application quality and transparency

Application Settings

Leave blank to keep the posting open indefinitely

Pre-screening questions help filter applicants before they apply

Intelligent Candidate Matching

Based on the job requirements you provide, our system will automatically evaluate applicants' resumes and qualifications. You'll receive match scores and rankings to help you identify the most qualified candidates quickly.
